DoubleO on the Low

DoubleO on the Low is a laid-back and insightful music talk show where artists, producers, and bands come together to discuss the sounds shaping Glasgow’s music scene. Hosted by Double O Productions, the show explores behind-the-scenes stories, songwriting processes, and plenty of hilarious gig moments — all in a relaxed, conversational setting.
